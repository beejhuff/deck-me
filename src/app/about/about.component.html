Additional Info:

This application is currently in Beta version 1.0. If you discover any bugs or unexpected behavior please feel free to email our front-end developer at joewparsley@gmail.com. Our application is powered by Magic the Gathering API, Angular CLI, Angular Material, and Firebase. We are committed to rolling out updates, bug fixes, and new features as often as we can to provide you with the best deck building experience.
