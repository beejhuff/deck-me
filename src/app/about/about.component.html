With Magic The Gathering being one of my favorite hobbies, I couldn't think of a better way to combine my personal interests with programing. This web site is a great example of things that I hope to make in the future. Simple applications with a simple and lite design.
Jared Jared, having been born in a desert, longed for the sea at a young age. At times, he would stand by the sink inside of his house and imagine a vast ocean, full of adventure and exploration. One day, while wearing his classic tri-corn hat and leather doublet, Jared sent out to accomplish his dream of becoming the first Pirate Lord. He then realized he was in a landlocked state. So after a quick google search, Jared discovered the second meaning of piracy. Immediately setting out to a small tech school, he then met his rival, Adrian Smith. After losing an arm wrestling match to the ham enthusiast, Jared has been focusing on honing his skills as a web developer in order to achieve his true dream, becoming the best pirate the world has ever known.
Adrian Adrian was born in a small town in Brutaslaviac. Between ages 5 and 5.5, Adrian was studying the vast concepts of computer science. His brain, being larger than most peoples, allowed Adrian to surpass his colleagues and be accepted to the University of Mensa at age 14. After shaming all those in Brakaslovo who questioned his knowledge, Adrian ventured off to America to face bigger challenges. There, at a small school in Portland, Adrian met his one and only rival..Jared Lotdkmeo. Adrian would continue on to defeat his rival by winning a arm wrestle match while simultaneously eating a ham sandwich. He also developed some of this application.
